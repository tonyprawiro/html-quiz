Quiz material are downloaded, processed, and stored in your local browser and no data is sent back to our server. No server-side technology to worry about.

Resume your progress

Sometimes you need to put your phone down and live your life. Your progress is automatically saved using HTML5 Storage API. Even if you restart your phone, you can still resume your progress.
